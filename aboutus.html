We,the Germantown Kids Cricket Club, GKCC, provide coaching for the youth to learn and improve their cricketing skills, with practices along with games on real cricket pitches. We play during parts of the spring, summer, and fall whenever the weather is appropriate.We practice Sunday mornings from 9-12 at Strawberry Knoll park @ 18810 Strawberry Knoll Rd, Gaithersburg, MD. We also compete in the Maryland Youth Cricket Cup every summer. We have both an under-15 and an under-11 team. All players are welcome!
